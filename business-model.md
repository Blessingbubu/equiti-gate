# Equiti Gate Business Model

## Mission

To make real estate investment accessible to everyone by allowing people to invest in professionally managed property projects with affordable entry amounts.

---

## Vision

To become one of the world's most trusted real estate investment platforms, enabling millions of people to build wealth through shared ownership of income-generating properties.

---

# How Equiti Gate Works

Equiti Gate pools investments from individuals around the world.

The company uses these funds to acquire, develop, and manage carefully selected real estate projects, including Airbnb properties, residential apartments, and commercial buildings.

Revenue generated from these properties is used to cover operating costs, grow the business, and distribute profits to eligible investors according to the platform's investment model.

# Property Strategy

Equiti Gate will focus on:

- Airbnb properties
- Holiday homes
- Residential apartments
- Commercial rental properties

Projects will be selected based on profitability, location, occupancy potential, and long-term appreciation.

---

# Revenue Sources

The company generates income through:

- Airbnb rental income
- Long-term rental income
- Property appreciation
- Management fees
- Strategic partnerships

---

# Transparency

Equiti Gate is committed to transparency by providing investors with access to:

- Property information
- Investment performance
- Portfolio tracking
- Transaction history
- Platform announcements

---

# Long-Term Goal

To build a global ecosystem where individuals can participate in real estate investment regardless of their location or starting capital.
